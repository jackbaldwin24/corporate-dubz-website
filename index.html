About

Corporate Dubz is a Chicago-based riddim DJ known for precise mixing, heavy selections, and a distinct visual identity that playfully flips corporate culture on its head. With a presence rooted in both humor and intensity, his sets bring structure and chaos into perfect balance.

He recently played direct support for Leotrix and Black Carl! on their tour stop in Detroit, and has opened for artists including VKTM, Joust, Subfiltronik, and Drippy. Whether he’s setting the tone or pushing the energy to the limit, Corporate Dubz stands out as a consistent force in the Midwest’s underground scene.
